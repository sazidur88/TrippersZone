1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

Our project is paying guest accommodation system. In our application users can search the nearest place for accommodation. Besides home searching in our application users can search other services like : restaurants, hospitals, police stations etc. House owners can give rent their houses and can get some profit. It will also solve hotel room booking crisis within a reasonable price.
